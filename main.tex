\section*{\begin{center}
      Chomsky Hierarchy
  \end{center}}

The image I have chosen is of a halved onion, where the rings of the onion can
be thought of as the \textbf{Chomsky Hierarchy} where each layer represents
different classes of Automata. The outermost layer, which is usually peeled off
while consuming the onion represents the \textit{Turing Unrecognizable} class
of Languages whereas the rest of the onion is consumable or \textit{Turing
    Recognizable}.
